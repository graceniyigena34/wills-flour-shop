Our process

From root to flour — in six honest steps.

See the full process →

Step 01

Harvesting

Mature cassava roots are hand-harvested from trusted farms.

Step 02

Peeling

Roots are carefully peeled to keep only the pure white flesh.

Step 03

Soaking

Soaked in clean water to remove natural compounds and impurities.

Step 04

Drying

Sun-dried on raised racks until perfectly crisp and ready.

Step 05

Grinding

Stone-milled into the soft, silky flour our customers love.

Step 06

Packaging

Sealed in food-safe bags for freshness from our mill to your kitchen.
